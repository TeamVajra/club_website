About Us

Team Vajra is the official Drone and Robotics Club of MMCOE, dedicated to pioneering innovation in UAV design, development, and aerial technology. Our team focuses on building cutting-edge autonomous systems, high-speed racing drones, and practical aerial solutions that push the boundaries of modern engineering.

Achievements: Proud winners of Technoxian 2025 — securing World Rank 3 and All India Rank 2 in Drone Racing, a testament to our technical mastery and collaborative spirit.

Driven by innovation, precision, and passion, Team Vajra continues to redefine the skies — one flight at a time — through excellence in engineering, teamwork, and relentless pursuit of aerial perfection.
