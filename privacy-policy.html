Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 23-02-2026

Welcome to Team Vajra. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy document explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

This policy applies to all visitors, users, and others who access our website.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information in the following ways:

1. Information You Provide

When you contact us through forms, email, or registration forms, we may collect information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Any message or details you submit

This information is used only to respond to your inquiries or provide requested services.

2. Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Pages visited
  • Date and time of visit
  • Referring website

This data helps us improve website performance and user experience.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us understand user behavior and improve our content.

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.

Google AdSense and Third-Party Advertising

We may use third-party advertising services such as Google AdSense to display advertisements.

Google uses cookies, including the DoubleClick cookie, to serve ads to users based on their visits to this and other websites.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting: https://www.google.com/settings/ads

Third-party ad servers or networks may use technologies like cookies, JavaScript, or web beacons in their advertisements.

We do not control these cookies used by third-party advertisers.

How We Use Your Information

We may use collected information to:

  • Improve our website content and user experience
  • Respond to inquiries and communication
  • Maintain website functionality
  • Analyze usage trends
  • Display relevant advertisements

We do not sell, trade, or rent users' personal information to third parties.

Data Protection

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Links to External Websites

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party sites.

We encourage users to review the privacy policies of those websites.

Children's Information

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data on our website, please contact us, and we will remove the information promptly.

Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

Updates to This Policy

Contact Us
